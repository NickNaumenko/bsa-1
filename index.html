Jeff Goins

I’m the best-selling author of five books including the The Art of Work and Real Artists Don’t Starve. On this blog, I share my thoughts on writing, life, and creative work. We all have a creative gift worth sharing with the world, and that is our art. So whether you have a business idea, a book in you, or some other project you want to start, my goal is to help you get that work out of you and into the world. And here’s the thing: you don’t have to starve to share your best work. If you have a passion for creativity and changing the world, this is the place for you. I invite you to subscribe and sit tight. This is going to be fun.
